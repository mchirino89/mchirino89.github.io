Breath provides guided one-minute breathing exercises. The Calm pattern is free forever. Additional patterns require a Breath Premium subscription.

## Subscriptions

- Payment is charged at confirmation of purchase. The subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
- You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
- Previous purchases can be restored with "Restore purchases" on the paywall.
